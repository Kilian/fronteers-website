     <h2>Fronteers 2009 venue</h2>
     <p class="figure side"><img src="/_img/congres/2009/venue/building.jpg" width="150" height="198" loading="lazy" decoding="async" alt=""></p>
     <p>Fronteers 2009 (5 and 6 November 2009) took place in <a href="http://felixmeritis.nl/">Felix Meritis</a>, a venerable 200-year-old venue that was originally a club for well-to-do 18th century science aficionados, then became the headquarters of the Dutch Communist Party for a while, and then a theatre and a conference venue.</p>
     <h3>Address</h3>
     <p>Felix Meritis<br>Keizersgracht 324<br>1016 EZ Amsterdam</p>
     <iframe width="640" height="320" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="//maps.google.nl/maps?q=Keizersgracht+324,+1016+EZ+Amsterdam&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=Keizersgracht+324,+1016+EZ+Amsterdam&amp;t=m&amp;z=15&amp;vpsrc=0&amp;output=embed"></iframe>
     <h3 id="route-description">Route description</h3>
     <h4>By public transport</h4>
     <ul>
      <li>From Central Station</li>
      <li>Tram 1, 2 or 5; stop Spui or</li>
      <li>Tram 13 or 17; stop Westermarkt</li>
     </ul>
     <h4>By car (not advised)</h4>
     <ul>
      <li>Ring road (A10 Ring West)</li>
      <li>Exit S105 (Geuzenveld)</li>
      <li>Drive in the direction of the centre (Jan van Galenstraat)</li>
      <li>Turn right at the Nassaukade</li>
      <li>Turn left at the traffic lights (Rozengracht)</li>
      <li>Turn right on the Westermarkt (Keizersgracht)</li>
      <li>You can park your car safely in car park Europarking, Marnixstraat 250 (a 5 minutes walk from Felix Meritis).</li>
     </ul>
    </div>
