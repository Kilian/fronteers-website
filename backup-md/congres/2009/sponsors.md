     <h2>Fronteers 2009 sponsorships</h2>
     <p>Fronteers, founded in 2007 as the professional union of Dutch front-end engineers, aims at increasing the knowledge and professionality of its members as well as front-end engineers in general. A conference is an excellent means to this end.</p>
     <p>The Fronteers 2008 conference was a huge success, and right now the organising committee is working on making Fronteers 2009 a worthy successor.</p>
     <p>Meanwhile Vodafone, Mirabeau, Microsoft, Opera, and Xopus have agreed to become main sponsors of this event; and this means the main sponsorships are sold out.</p>
     <p>Nonetheless the organisation has noted that there is a lively interest in sponsoring Fronteers 2009. Therefore we offer the minor sponsorships described below. We aim at about four to five minor sponsors.</p>
     <h3>Facts</h3>
     <ul>
      <li>Date: 5th and 6th of November, 2009</li>
      <li>Venue: Felix Meritis, Amsterdam</li>
      <li>Ticket price: 350 euros; see also the <a href="/congres/2009/tickets">pricing scheme</a></li>
     </ul>
     <h3>Target audience</h3>
     <p>Fronteers 2009 exclusively aims at front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). As a result the target audience consists wholly of front-end engineers.</p>
     <p>We ask all our speakers to dig deep into a technical subject of their choice, and thus we sacrifice breadth to attain depth; something that's rarely done at international web conferences and was received very well in 2008.</p>
     <p>At the time of writing about 160 tickets have been sold, and altogether we expect about 250 to 300 visitors. A small majority comes from the Netherlands, but Belgium, Germany, England, Scandinavia and several other countries are also represented.</p>
     <p>See also the <a href="/congres/2009/attendees">attendee list</a>.</p>
     <h3>Minor sponsorship</h3>
     <p>Cost: 1000 euros, to which 19% Dutch VAT will be added.</p>
     <h4>Benefits:</h4>
     <ul>
      <li>Website and booklet will show the sponsor's logo (though less prominently than the major sponsors' logos)</li>
      <li>Every sponsor can add something to the conference bag.</li>
      <li>The sponsor has the option of showing a short film on the LCD screens that are present in the break area. (Minor sponsors will be allotted less time than major ones.)</li>
      <li>All badges of all attendees that work for the sponsor, will be branded with the sponsor's logo. Thus the sponsor and its employees become easily recognisable for other conference attendees.</li>
     </ul>
     <h3>Contact</h3>
     <p>For more information on the conference or sponsorships, contact</p>
