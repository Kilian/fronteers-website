     <h2>Announcing 2 workshops and 9 speakers for #fronteers15</h2>
     <p class="note">Posted on May 28, 2015</p>
     <p>With four months to go until the conference, and on the eve of our first batch of ticket sales, it's our pleasure to announce the first set of confirmed speakers for Fronteers 2015... And two great pre-conference workshops!</p>
     <h3>Speaker names</h3>
     <p>Head on over to our updated <a href="/congres/2015/speakers">Fronteers 2015 Speakers page</a>, which now includes bios and suave profile pictures of:</p>
     <ul>
      <li>Rachel Andrew</li>
      <li>Wes Bos</li>
      <li>Espen Brunborg</li>
      <li>Chris Eppstein</li>
      <li>Scott Jehl</li>
      <li>Gordon MacLachlan</li>
      <li>Bart Oleszczyk</li>
      <li>Soledad Penadés</li>
      <li>Remy Sharp</li>
     </ul>
     <p>We'll be adding a Sessions page in the coming months to provide you with hype-building information on their talks.</p>
     <h3>Pre-conference Workshops</h3>
     <p>Additionally, we're proud to announce two full-day, deep-dive, bang-on, well-catered, pre-conference workshops for Wednesday, October 7, the day before the conf. Wes Bos and Remy Sharp will cover workflow and debugging topics, respectively, in their in-demand workshops. Tickets for the workshops will be €345, or €270 for Fronteers members (in both cases, + 21% Dutch VAT) go on sale tomorrow, together with the first batch of conference tickets. Take a look at the updated Fronteers 2015 Workshop pages for <a href="/congres/2015/workshops/workshop-mastering-devtools-remy-sharp">Remy Sharp on Mastering browser devtools</a> and <a href="/congres/2015/workshops/workshop-mastering-gulp-js-by-wes-bos">Wes Bos on Mastering Gulp Build Systems</a> for more information.</p>
     <h3>Ticket Sales</h3>
     <p>Tomorrow evening at 20:00 Amsterdam time, ticket sales will start. Head on over to <a href="https://tickets.fronteers.nl">https://tickets.fronteers.nl</a> to secure yours. As always, take a look at <a href="https://twitter.com/FronteersConf">@FronteersConf</a> for all the latest -- and see you in October!</p>
