     <h2>#fronteers15 ticket sales to start Friday, May 29</h2>
     <p class="note">Posted on May 15, 2015</p>
     <p>Mark your calendars, set your alarms and preload your browsers. Tickets for the 2015 Fronteers conference will go on sale two weeks from today. We're doing things a little differently this year - here's what will happen.</p>
     <h4>TL;DR</h4>
     <ul>
      <li>Start of sales: May 29, 2015</li>
      <li>Batched sales</li>
      <li>No early-bird discount</li>
      <li>Lower ticket prices for all</li>
      <li>Discount for Fronteers members</li>
     </ul>
     <h4>Ticket prices</h4>
     <p>We're cutting out the early-bird ticket type in favour of a single, lower ticket price for everyone. Fronteers 2015 tickets will sell for €345 excluding 21% Dutch VAT. Those of you who are members of the Fronteers professional association on or before May 14, are eligible for a €75 discount and can buy a ticket for €270 excluding VAT.</p>
     <h4>Batched sales</h4>
     <p>If you happen to be on holiday when tickets go on sale, don't worry - we're going to sell the tickets in three batches and you can get a ticket at the same price as everyone else. The first batch of 300 tickets goes on sale on Friday, May 29 at 20:00 CEST. The next batches will be announced well ahead of time.</p>
     <p>Go to tickets.fronteers.nl on Friday the 29th to claim your tickets!</p>
     <h4>About #fronteers15</h4>
     <p class="note">Fronteers conference is one of Europe’s largest conferences on front-end web development, organised in Amsterdam yearly since 2008. The eighth edition of our single-track community conference will be held in the beautiful Pathé Tuschinski theatre on Thursday and Friday, October 8-9, 2015. A complementary day of hands-on workshops is scheduled for Wednesday, October 7.</p>
