     <h2>2013 Jam Session registrations open + call for speakers</h2>
     <p>Traditionally, on Wednesday evening before the conference, Fronteers opens the floor to <em>you</em> for a round of low-pressure, high-fun 10-minute front-end jam sessions. Obviously, this is not a tradition we're eager to break, so the conference organisation is happy to announce that the Fronteers Conference Jam Session 2013 is open for registration.</p>
     <p>The 2013 pre-conference Jam Session will be held on <em>Wednesday, October 9</em>, at the wonderful Kapitein Zeppos venue in the heart of Amsterdam.</p>
     <p>If you're an attendee, come join (and/or support) 200 of your peers, by <a href="/congres/2013/jam-session">registering on the 2013 Jam Session page</a>. The schedule has also been opened for talk proposals, so if there's a front-end topic you've been eager to address, submit your proposal now!</p>
     <p>Check out <a href="/congres/2012/jam-session">last year's talks</a> on the 2012 Jam Session page and then quickly head over to <a href="/congres/2013/jam-session">the 2013 Jam Session page</a> to register for fun, drinks and exciting talks.</p>
