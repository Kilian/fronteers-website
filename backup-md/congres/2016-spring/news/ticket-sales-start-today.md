     <h2>Fronteers Spring Conference ticket sales start today!</h2>
     <p class="note">Posted on February 12, 2016</p>
     <p>Ticket sales for Fronteers Spring Conference start today at 12:00 CET: get yours at <a href="https://tickets.fronteers.nl">https://tickets.fronteers.nl</a> if you're a Fronteers member.</p>
     <p>It's a new, single-day front-end conference scheduled for Friday, April 1. The event will take place on Amsterdam's waterfront at the city's beautiful film museum, EYE.</p>
     <p>We've gathered bright minds from around the globe to provide you with thought-provoking talks and insights. If you love our autumnal sibling Fronteers Conference, we think you're going to love Spring Conference, too.</p>
     <h3>Conference Theme: Web Performance</h3>
     <p>The overarching theme for our 2016 Spring Conference is Web Performance. We'll cover web perf in-depth, and from a variety of perspectives. The day will consist of multiple sets of three 20 minute talks, delivered back-to-back(-to-back), followed by an MC-led panel discussion for each set.</p>
     <h3>Speakers &amp; MC</h3>
     <p>We're very proud that two-time Fronteers Conference alum, tall person and all-around front-end development expert <strong>Phil Hawksworth</strong> has agreed to be our Master of Ceremonies for the day. Phil will be telling you where the exits are, as well as conducting panel discussions with our speakers.</p>
     <p>Some of the Web's best and brightest will come to Amsterdam to think, talk and discuss Web Perf topics. We're delighted to announce the first set of speakers:</p>
     <ul>
      <li>Tobias Baldauf</li>
      <li>Marcy Sutton</li>
      <li>Karl Groves</li>
      <li>Tobias Ahlin</li>
      <li>Paul Bakaus</li>
      <li>Bram Stein</li>
      <li>Yan Zhu</li>
      <li>Estelle Weyl</li>
     </ul>
     <p>Find out more about our growing speaker list, the speakers and their talks at the <a href="/congres/2016-spring/speakers">Fronteers Spring Conference speaker page</a>.</p>
     <h3>Venue: EYE</h3>
     <p>Fronteers Spring Conference 2016 will be held at EYE, the Netherlands' film museum, in its mighty Cinema 1 theatre. EYE is located on the waterfront, across Amsterdam's Central Station, in a spectacular building.</p>
